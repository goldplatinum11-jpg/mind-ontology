# Glossary

## Lumen #term #product

Our product family. Two lines: Lumen Core (free, open source) and Lumen Cloud
(paid, hosted). "Lumen" alone usually means the whole family — say Core or Cloud
when the line actually matters.

## Luminary #term #competitor

A competing product. Not ours and not affiliated. Never describe Lumen features
using "Luminary" naming, and never borrow their wording in our docs or copy.

## Workspace #term #product

In Lumen, a Workspace is one customer's isolated tenant inside Cloud. It is not a
folder, an editor window, or a generic "workspace" — use the word only for the
billed tenant.

## Open-core #term #boundary

Our business model: the engine is open source (Core) and the managed hosting plus
team features are paid (Cloud). The line between the two is a hard boundary, not a
suggestion.
