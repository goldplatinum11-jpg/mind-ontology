# Direction

## Grow Lumen Cloud revenue #direction #now #hosted

The near-term priority is paid conversion on the hosted product, Lumen Cloud.
Work that improves onboarding, billing, or team features for paying customers
moves this forward.

## Keep Lumen Core healthy and open #direction #oss

The free, self-hosted engine, Lumen Core, must stay genuinely useful on its own.
Community trust depends on the open-source line not being hollowed out to push
people toward Cloud.

## Protect founder focus #direction #operations

Reduce context-switching across projects. Automate reporting and triage so the
operator spends time on product and customers, not coordination overhead.
