# Direction

## Ship the Acme retail assistant #direction #now #client-acme

The near-term focus is the Acme retail assistant v1: a product-search and
returns helper. Move this engagement forward; it is the most time-sensitive
client right now.

## Roll out the Northwind clinic scheduler #direction #client-northwind

For Northwind, move the patient self-scheduling flow toward a careful rollout.
Health-adjacent data means this engagement advances more conservatively than the
others — correctness and privacy before speed.

## Advance the Globex trading copilot #direction #client-globex

For Globex, deepen the analyst research copilot while keeping it strictly
read-only against market data. Move this engagement forward without ever placing
or simulating a trade.

## Keep client engagements isolated #direction #isolation

Every client's data, context, and deliverables stay inside that client's
workspace. Isolation is a first-class goal, not an afterthought — cross-client
leakage is the failure this studio most wants to avoid.

## Standardize the studio platform #direction #studio-platform

Factor the parts that every engagement shares — auth, logging, the context
compiler wiring — into the studio platform so each new client starts from a
proven base instead of a copy-paste.
