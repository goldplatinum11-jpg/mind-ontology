# Glossary

## Context pack #context #term

A task-scoped bundle of direction, decisions, constraints, and vocabulary that
an AI agent receives before acting.

## Constraint #constraint #term

A non-negotiable rule the agent must respect. Constraints are included even
when the task does not appear to match them.

## Decision #decision #term

A durable choice and reason that should guide future work unless it is replaced
by a newer decision.

## Hosted adapter #adapter #term

An optional connection from the local ontology to a hosted memory, graph, or
writeback service. The local ontology should still work without it.
